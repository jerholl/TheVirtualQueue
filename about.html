Behind the Virtual Queue

My name is Jeremy. I've been a fan of theme parks for almost 10 years now. (Man time flies when you're having fun!) I love writing, filming, and editing and I love discussing theme parks so I decided, why not combine them? So here I am, wanting to bring great stories to you, from me. Thanks for stopping by!

The Mission

Our goal is to provide condensed, accurate information in a quick-read format.

Why It Matters

People like small tidbits of information as they go about their day and don't always have time to read long articles. So here at The Virtual Queue, we condense long articles for quick reads.
